Career

Jeetendar Srivastav has been a vibrant presence in Bollywood since 1997.

He began as an assistant director in the Maithili film Kakhan Harab Dukh Mor (2005). [IMDb]

In 2014, he produced the Hindi feature film Life Mein Twist Hai. [IMDb]

In 2017, he directed the music video for Zee Music's album "Heeriye". [YouTube] [JioSaavn]

In 2020, he directed the film Womanhood, a bold take on women-centric issues.
